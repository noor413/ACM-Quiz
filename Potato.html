The new milk alternative is made from potatoes

Price PKR- 40/kg

A Swedish company are introducing potato milk as a new “super-sustainable ” member to the plant-based ‘milk ’family.Plant-based ‘milk’ has become a staple in the food industry with products made from oats, almonds and rice now readily available. However, some of these crops can be resource intensive. Looking for a more sustainable and easily harvested alternative, Swedish brand Dug has turned to potatoes. That’s right, potato ‘milk’ is heading our way.
